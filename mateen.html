About Polarion ALM

Polarion ALM (Application Lifecycle Management) is a comprehensive platform that supports the entire software development lifecycle. It allows teams to manage requirements, track changes, conduct testing, and ensure compliance, all within a centralized and collaborative environment. By integrating various development processes and providing real-time traceability, Polarion ALM enhances productivity and helps organizations maintain high-quality standards in software delivery.

Core Functionality

Polarion® REQUIREMENTS™ ensures that software products meet the highest standards through structured requirements management, test planning, and defect tracking. It provides complete traceability by linking requirements to test cases, risks, and change requests, ensuring every aspect of quality is monitored and validated.

Teams can create custom workflows, automate processes, and track changes in real-time, reducing errors and improving efficiency. With role-based access, version control, and compliance tracking, organizations can uphold quality standards while adhering to industry regulations.

The platform integrates with testing tools, CI/CD pipelines, and reporting dashboards, offering valuable insights into requirement coverage, defect trends, and overall product quality. Through customizable dashboards and analytics, teams can proactively identify risks and optimize their strategies.

Requirements Management

Polarion® REQUIREMENTS™ ensures that all project requirements are clearly defined, traceable, and aligned with quality objectives. It allows teams to capture, organize, and manage requirements in a centralized system, linking them to test cases, defects, and risk assessments.

With version control, automated change tracking, and real-time collaboration, teams can manage evolving requirements while maintaining compliance. Custom workflows, live dashboards, and audit trails enforce standardization and monitor validation.

Integration with test management and defect tracking allows QA teams to identify gaps and verify compliance, minimizing risks and enhancing requirement accuracy for superior product quality.

Change & Configuration Management

Polarion® REQUIREMENTS™ ensures that all modifications tied to requirements are tracked and validated. Teams can manage change requests, monitor version history, and enforce approval workflows.

Full traceability, automated change tracking, and impact analysis assess the effects of changes on requirements, test cases, and defects, minimizing error risks.

Integration with repositories like Git and Subversion ensures consistency across environments, supporting quality assurance best practices.

Test & Quality Management

Polarion® REQUIREMENTS™ offers a structured approach to ensuring software reliability and compliance by linking testing to requirements. It enables teams to plan, execute, and track test cases while maintaining full traceability with requirements and defects.

Teams can define custom workflows, automate defect tracking, and generate real-time reports, allowing them to monitor test coverage and ensure compliance with industry standards. Integration with CI/CD tools like Jenkins enhances testing efficiency.

Customizable dashboards provide insights into defect trends, test execution status, and risk analysis, helping optimize testing strategies for high-quality software releases.

Issue & Risk Management

Polarion® REQUIREMENTS™ provides a traceable approach to identifying, tracking, and resolving defects tied to requirements. Teams can log, prioritize, and assign issues with full traceability to test cases and code changes.

Customizable workflows and automated notifications ensure critical issues are addressed promptly. Integration with tools like Jira and Git enhances efficiency in defect resolution.

Dashboards and analytics offer insights into defect trends, severity levels, and resolution times, helping teams improve software quality and streamline QA processes.

Agile & Hybrid Management

In summary, Polarion ALM is a powerful solution for Agile and Hybrid methodologies, offering customizable templates, real-time collaboration, and comprehensive traceability. Whether you’re fully Agile or blending approaches, Polarion adapts to your needs, helping teams deliver faster while maintaining quality and compliance.

Benefits for Agile/Hybrid in Polarion ALM

  • Speed and Flexibility: Agile teams benefit from rapid iteration and adaptability, while Hybrid teams gain structured planning without losing agility.
  • Compliance and Quality: Built-in traceability, electronic signatures, and audit trails ensure regulatory compliance (e.g., ISO 26262, FDA standards), critical for Hybrid projects in regulated sectors.
  • Transparency: Real-time data aggregation aligns stakeholders around project goals, whether Agile or Hybrid.
  • Tool Integration: Polarion integrates with tools like Jira, Azure DevOps, and MATLAB Simulink, supporting Hybrid workflows where teams use different systems.

Build & Release

Build and Release management in Polarion ALM (Application Lifecycle Management) is a critical part of its capability to streamline the software development lifecycle, ensuring that code is built, tested, and deployed efficiently. Polarion ALM integrates these processes into its unified platform, providing traceability, automation, and visibility for teams practicing Agile, Waterfall, or Hybrid methodologies.

AI-powered integrations further enhance decision-making by providing predictive insights and intelligent automation.
